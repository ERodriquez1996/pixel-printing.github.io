Pupusas

Featured Item

Pupusas are thick corn tortillas that can be stuffed with various fillings, including various meats, refried beans, cheese, and even squash flowers. The filled corn tortillas are then cooked to perfection on a traditional grill referred to as a comal.

Tamales

Tamales are a traditional Mexican dish made with a corn based dough mixture that is filled with various meats or beans and cheese. Tamales are wrapped and cooked in corn husks or banana leaves, but they are removed from the husks before eating. Try them served with pico de gallo on top and a side of guacamole and rice.

Torta

A Mexican torta resembles a sandwich in almost every way : a crusty roll is sliced in half and filled with a variety of meats and other ingredients. However, what goes inside a torta is uniquely Mexican and includes a variety of bold and spicy sauces and garnishes that are found in many other types of Mexican dishes

Elote Loco

Elote Loco is made with fresh corn on the cob that is typically grilled or boiled. The corn is then slathered with a mixture of mayonnaise, crema (Mexican sour cream) , and cheese. The final touch is a sprinkle of chili powder and a squeeze of lime juice, adding a burst of tangy and spicy flavors

Carne Asada

The meat is characteristically marinated in lime juice, salt, and Mexican seasonings, but may also be simply rubbed with salt or spice rubs such as lemon pepper, before grilled. After grilling it is typically chopped for filling tacos and burritos, which also minimizes toughness. Once grilled, it is called carne asada.
